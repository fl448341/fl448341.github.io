Baltic Defense

The Baltic Defense is a chess opening that begins with the moves 1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nc6 3. Bb5. This opening is characterized by Black’s early development of the knight to c6, challenging the center and preparing to defend the e5 pawn. The Baltic Defense is a solid and flexible opening that allows Black to take the initiative and fight for control of the center. It is a popular choice for players looking to counter White’s aggressive opening moves and develop a strong position.
