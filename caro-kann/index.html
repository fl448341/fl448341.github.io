Chess Openings

Caro-Kann

The Caro-Kann defense is a solid chess opening that begins with the moves 1. e4 c6. Black’s goal is to create a stable pawn center and prepare counterattacking possibilities. After 2. d4 d5, Black has a number of options, including the Classical Variation (3. exd5 cxd5), the Advance Variation (3. Nc3 dxe4 4. Nxe4), and the Exchange Variation (3. exd5 cxd5). The Caro-Kann is considered a reliable defense against 1. e4, offering Black the chance to equalize or even gain the initiative with careful play.
