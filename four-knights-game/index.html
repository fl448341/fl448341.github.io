Chess Openings

Four Knights Game

The Four Knights Game is a chess opening that begins with the moves 1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nc6 3. Nc3. This opening is characterized by the rapid development of both players’ knight pieces, leading to a complex and dynamic position. The opening often transposes into other openings, such as the Ruy Lopez or the Italian Game, depending on the subsequent moves. The Four Knights Game is considered a solid and flexible opening choice for White, providing opportunities for both positional and tactical play.
