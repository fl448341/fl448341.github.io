Gianutio Counter Gambit

The Gianutio Counter Gambit is a chess opening that begins with the moves: 1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nc6 3. Bc4 Bc5 4. O-O Nf6 5. d3. This opening is characterized by Black’s immediate counterattack against White’s center, aiming to undermine White’s pawn on e4. The Gianutio Counter Gambit is an aggressive and unbalanced opening that can lead to sharp and tactical positions, as Black seeks to seize the initiative and create threats against White’s king. It is named after the Italian chess player Gianutio, who popularized this opening in the 19th century.
