Chess Openings

Italian Game

The Italian Game is a popular chess opening that begins with the moves 1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nc6 3. Bc4. This opening is characterized by an early development of the white bishop to the c4 square, putting pressure on the center and the e5 pawn. The Italian Game often leads to sharp, tactical positions, with both players aiming to control the center and launch attacks on the opposing king. The opening is considered sound and has been played by many strong grandmasters throughout the history of chess.
