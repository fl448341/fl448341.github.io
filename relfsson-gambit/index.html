Relfsson Gambit

The Relfsson Gambit is a chess opening that begins with the moves 1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.d4 exd4 4.Bb5. This gambit is characterized by White’s aggressive bishop deployment to b5, immediately pressuring Black’s knight on c6 and indirectly challenging control over the center. White sacrifices the pawn on d4 to accelerate piece development, aiming to rapidly seize the initiative and create tactical opportunities. The Relfsson Gambit typically results in dynamic and open positions, making it appealing to players who enjoy sharp, active play and early attacking possibilities.
