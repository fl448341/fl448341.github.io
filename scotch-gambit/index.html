Scotch Gambit

The Scotch Gambit is a chess opening that begins with the moves 1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.d4 exd4 4.Bc4. This opening features White's aggressive bishop development to c4, immediately targeting Black's vulnerable f7-square and striving for rapid piece activity and control of the center. By sacrificing the pawn on d4, White seeks dynamic piece play, quick development, and tactical attacking opportunities against Black's position. The Scotch Gambit typically leads to sharp, open positions, attracting players who enjoy early initiative and tactical complexity in the opening phase.
